Top Areas to Stay in Haridwar
Different areas of Haridwar have different benefits. Therefore, selecting the right place depends on the reason for your trip.

Har Ki Pauri is the very popular ghat in Haridwar. People come here for Ganga Aarti and take bath. Therefore, staying near this area is best for visitors who want to stay close to the spiritual center. For better comfort, travelers can also explore stay arrangements during the Kumbh Mela season.

Near Railway Station
This area is good for budget travelers and short visits. Moreover, hotels here are easy to reach and helpful for people coming by train.

Near Rishikesh Road
This area has quiet hotels and resorts. Moreover, it is good for families who want a peaceful stay away from the crowd.

Near Main Market

Best Time to Reach Haridwar
Haridwar can be visited every year, but some seasons are better for comfort.

During Kumbh Mela
This is the most special time for spiritual visitors. During this time, the city becomes full of energy, devotion, and religious activities.
